The Haunted Kirti Mandir

Kirti Mandir, located in the heart of Vadodara, is said to be haunted by the spirits of the past. The temple, built in honor of the great leader, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, is a peaceful place during the day, but as night falls, a chilling atmosphere surrounds it. Many visitors have reported feeling watched and hearing strange whispers when they are alone.

I visited the Kirti Mandir one evening, curious about the rumors surrounding the place. As I stood near the entrance, the temperature suddenly dropped. The air felt heavier, as though something unseen was present. I heard soft footsteps behind me, but when I turned, no one was there.

Faint, hollow voices seemed to echo through the temple's halls. There was no way to explain the strange sounds coming from within. People passing by mentioned how they had seen shadows moving within the temple, but upon entering, there was nothing but emptiness.

Since that night, I haven’t been able to shake off the feeling that the Kirti Mandir is not as peaceful as it seems. The spirits of the past still linger, their presence felt in the eerie silence that blankets the area after dusk.
