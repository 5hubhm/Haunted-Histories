The Haunted Ajwa Water Park

Ajwa Water Park, one of Vadodara’s most popular leisure spots, is rumored to be haunted. Located away from the city’s hustle, this water park once bustled with visitors, but many began to notice strange occurrences in its secluded corners, especially at night.

It is said that after sunset, some of the water rides would suddenly start operating on their own. The sound of water splashing could be heard, and sometimes, faint figures were seen in the distance—figures that would vanish upon a closer look.

One night, I decided to investigate. As I walked along the now-deserted pathways, a cold breeze swept past me. I heard a loud splash from the pool, yet no one was around. When I approached, I felt an overwhelming sense of unease, as if someone—or something—was watching me.

Visitors have mentioned hearing children laughing and playing, even though there are no children in sight. The haunted reputation of Ajwa Water Park has persisted, making it a place for thrill-seekers looking for a real-life horror experience.
